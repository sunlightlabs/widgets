Politiwidgets

Politiwidgets was a free political widget service, created by the Sunlight Foundation. It has now been retired to a gentle pasture in a rural computing cloud. Check out our other transparency projects to find more tools and information about your government.

Sunlight Foundation

The Sunlight Foundation uses cutting-edge technology and ideas to make government transparent and accountable. Through its projects and grant-making, Sunlight serves as a catalyst to create greater political transparency and to foster more openness and accountability in government.
